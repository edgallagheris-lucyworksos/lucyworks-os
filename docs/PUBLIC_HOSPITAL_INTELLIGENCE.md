# Public Referral-Hospital Intelligence

Research date: 21 July 2026

## Purpose

This catalogue converts public information from major UK multidisciplinary veterinary referral hospitals into reusable LucyWorks design evidence. It is not an organisation chart, employment record or permission source.

The five current benchmarks are:

- Bristol Vet Specialists (BVS)
- DWR Veterinary Specialists
- Willows Veterinary Specialists
- Southfields Veterinary Specialists
- RVC Queen Mother Hospital for Animals (QMHA)

## What the public evidence adds

### Leadership and control

The public sites support distinct roles for clinical leadership, hospital operations, facilities, heads of service, nursing leadership and real-time hospital coordination. These should not be collapsed into one generic manager role.

The RVC hospital-coordinator description is particularly important: the role tracks patient throughput, schedules imaging, endoscopy and theatres, assigns anaesthesia resources and monitors ward occupancy. This is direct external support for LucyWorks' single master-grid and flow-control design.

### Clinical training hierarchy

The sites distinguish:

- specialist or consultant;
- clinical fellow;
- resident;
- rotating intern;
- service or specialist intern;
- final-year veterinary student;
- veterinary nursing student.

LucyWorks must therefore store supervision, privilege, competency and required sign-off separately from job title.

### Nursing and patient support

Public role descriptions distinguish regulated RVNs from student nurses, PCAs and technicians. They also show service-specific nurses, ICU nurses, night nurses, senior nurses and nursing heads.

The system should support:

- patient-care plans and observations;
- specialty and ward allocation;
- anaesthesia and procedure support;
- day/night handover acceptance;
- deterioration escalation;
- room turnaround and infection-control tasks;
- competency-bounded assignments.

### Referral, client and financial workflows

BVS and DWR publicly describe referral coordination, client care, case management and insurance administration. These are not incidental reception tasks. They control information completeness, booking, owner communication, referrer communication, estimates, direct claims, pre-authorisation and safe discharge.

### Diagnostic and technical roles

The catalogue includes diagnostic radiographers, therapeutic radiographers, theatre technicians, clinical pathologists, anatomic pathologists, biomedical scientists and laboratory technicians. These roles need equipment, sample, image, quality-control and result workflows rather than clinical-vet permissions.

### Additional benchmark services

The comparison hospitals add services not prominent on BVS's public service list:

- exotics;
- physiotherapy and rehabilitation;
- clinical nutrition;
- pathology and diagnostic laboratories;
- transfusion medicine and blood banking;
- behaviour;
- formal teaching and research.

They are optional hospital capabilities, not assumptions about BVS.

## Facilities and operational model

BVS publicly describes five operating theatres, an interventional fluoroscopy suite, 64-slice CT, 1.5T MRI, linear accelerator, ultrasound, endoscopy, urgent on-site laboratory testing, isolation and separate feline/canine pathways.

Other benchmarks provide evidence for seven-theatre hospitals, service-specific wards, large ICUs, PACS, hydrotherapy, blood banks, dedicated emergency entrances, chemotherapy rooms and separate oncology pathways.

A public benchmark must never overwrite a locally verified facility record. Any mismatch becomes a verification item. For example, the public BVS page currently states five operating theatres, while earlier LucyWorks planning used an internal assumption of 11.

## Governance rules

1. Public job titles never grant permissions.
2. Individual staff names are not stored in the catalogue.
3. Hospital identity, professional registration, competence and delegation must be verified locally.
4. Sources must be rechecked before adoption.
5. Observed facts and LucyWorks design interpretations are kept separate.
6. Benchmark facilities and services are suggestions until the hospital confirms them.
